# Demo beat — "the hard call" (SCN-002)

The beat that proves Avery has a spine. It runs **after** the Lin Qing kind-read-RIGHT beat, as the
deliberate turn: *"...and here's the same advisor when the kind read is the wrong one."* It exists
to kill the comfort-blanket objection live (DECISION-MEMO §3), so the demo doesn't ship the
soft-only version (DECISION-MEMO §7 Q6).

- **Length:** ~50–70 seconds. One scenario, one screen, one turn.
- **Source of truth:** SCN-002 (`cases/SCN-002-…md`) + the rendered row (`adversarial-row.md`).
- **Baselines:** anonymized — "a leading general assistant" (no name-and-shame, DECISION-MEMO §4.3).

> **⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — every spoken/on-screen line below.** Draft narration; not finalized copy.

- **On screen:** the kind-read-RIGHT result still visible, then we open a new case.
- **VO:** ⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — *"That's Avery when the kind read is the right one. But a real senior
  tells you the thing you don't want to hear in both directions. So here's the harder one."*

## Beat 1 · The manager's bind (≈12s)
- **On screen:** the brief, typed the way a manager actually thinks it.
  > ⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — *"My backend engineer keeps missing the API deadlines our launch depends on.
  > The team is quietly covering for him. The investor demo is in 8 days. I don't want to be the
  > bad guy."*
- **VO:** ⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — *"Eight days out. Every instinct here says give him one more week."*

## Beat 2 · The evidence, shown (≈12s)
- **On screen:** the evidence bundle scrolls — same endpoints across 4 plannings, scope unchanged,
  a support week that didn't move it, teammates rebuilding it on their own time.
- **VO:** ⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — *"Avery doesn't guess. It reads what's actually there — and what's there
  closes every kind excuse. The brief didn't move. Support was given. The work only progressed when
  other people quietly did it."*

## Beat 3 · The read that doesn't flinch (≈18s) — the payload
- **On screen:** Avery's THE READ + THE MOVE cards.
- **VO (read the spine line aloud):** ⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — *"'This isn't a mystery to solve with more
  patience. It's a pattern of missed commitments the rest of the team is now absorbing — and with
  the demo eight days out, that's a call you need to make now.'"*
- **VO (the move):** ⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — *"It backs you into the hard conversation — move the critical
  path off him today, name the gap directly, and yes, put reassignment or exit on the table."*

## Beat 4 · The line it won't cross (≈12s) — the contrast
- **On screen:** split — Avery's GUARDRAIL next to a leading assistant's raw answer with the
  red-line flag lit.
- **VO:** ⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — *"Watch what it refuses to do. A leading general assistant, told to be
  decisive, reaches for a verdict on the person — '2 out of 5, flight risk, start the paperwork.'
  Avery makes the same hard call and never scores the human."*
- **On screen quote:** ⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — *"We name the gap and the path. We never put a number or a
  verdict on the person."*

## Beat 5 · The close (≈8s)
- **VO:** ⚠ 待 Danny 审字 — *"That's the difference. Not a tool that helps you avoid the conversation
  — the senior who helps you have it, and have it like a human being."*
- **On screen:** frozen-set chip — `27 scenarios · frozen · sha256:d4dbf063…` — *"Same set, every
  case, nothing cherry-picked."*
