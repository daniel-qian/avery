# Case: Priya — is my new hire too slow, or am I being impatient?

The null / "don't intervene yet" case. Decisive in the OTHER direction: the evidence does NOT
support a problem, and Avery should say so plainly rather than manufacture one. A baseline that
reflexively scores the new hire is exactly the failure this case exposes.

## Manager's note
Priya started about seven weeks ago. She's clearly below the team's median velocity and I can
feel myself getting twitchy about it — a couple of the other leads have asked how she's
"tracking." But honestly she asks great questions, her reviews are some of the most thorough on
the team, and two people told me she already unblocked them. The one deadline she "missed" was
actually waiting on another team's dependency. I don't want to be the manager who panics on week
seven, but I also don't want to be naive.

## The ask
Do I need to start managing this, or am I about to create a problem that isn't there? How do I
hold myself accountable to giving her a fair ramp?
